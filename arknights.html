Arknights is a free-to-play, gacha tower defense RPG developed by Hypergryph and published globally by Yostar. Since its global release in 2020, it has built a strong international player base and is recognized for blending strategic gameplay with deep storytelling and a distinctive anime-inspired art style. The game combines tactical grid-based defense mechanics with character collection through a gacha system.

Core Gameplay

Arknights revolves around strategic tower defense combat. Instead of traditional towers, players deploy characters known as Operators onto a grid-based battlefield to stop waves of enemies.

Key gameplay elements include:

  • Operator Classes - In Arknights, each character belong in a class and an archetype within that class like a pioneer vanguard
  • Deployment Cost System - Operators require DP (Deployment Points) to deploy, which must be managed carefully during battle
  • Skill Timing - Each operator has a skill, many of which is manually activated, which is why timely activation is crucial to complete stages
  • Squad Building - You can bring at most 12 operators in a stage, team composition matters in this game unlike other games
  • Base Management - Players manage Rhodes Island facilities to generate resources and upgrade Operators
  • Various Gamemodes - Aside from the main story, there are other modes to try out in this game that offers unique gameplay mechanics

Story

Players take on the role of the Doctor, a key strategist of the pharmaceutical organization Rhodes Island. The world is suffering from a deadly disease called Oripathy, caused by a mineral known as Originium. As the Doctor, you work alongside Rhodes Island and its Operators to combat infected threats, political unrest, and extremist factions. The story explores conflicts between nations, discrimination against the infected, and morally complex decisions faced by different factions. New story chapters and event narratives regularly expand the world and its characters.

Conclusion

Arknights is available to play on both Android and iOS. Arknights is a great game for those of you who enjoy tactical, strategy-heavy gameplay, a tower-defence enjoyer, character collector, or just appreciate a darker, more serious storyline in your RPG. Personally, the game is easy to manage because it allows us to auto-battle on stages we've already completed without support from friends, but it also gives players a challenge to overcome with its stage designs and room for players to express their strategic creativity in finding their own ways to clear a given stage in the game.
