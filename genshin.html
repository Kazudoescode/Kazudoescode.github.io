Genshin Impact is a free-to-play, open-world, Gacha action-RPG developed and published by HoYoverse. Released in 2020, it quickly became one of the most successful global RPG launches, surpassing 100 million downloads across platforms. It features fantasy elements, engaging combat, and compelling characters and storylines.

Core Gameplay

In Genshin Impact, you engage in real-time action combat where players control a party of four characters (one active at a time). Unlike turn-based systems, combat happens live, requiring timing, positioning, and quick reactions.

Key gameplay feautures:

  • Elemental Combat System - Characters wield elements such as Pyro, Hydro, Electro, Cryo, and more. Combining elements triggers elemental reactions. Interacting with these reactions is the core part of Genshin's combat sysstem
  • Characters - Each character offers unique skills, elemental abilities and burst attacks.
  • Gacha System - You can obtain characters and weapons through the gacha banners
  • Exploration - another of Genshin's core aspects is it's beautiful sceneries and open-world exploration where you go around the world whether to explore, uncover secrets, discover treasures, and even foraging for various materials used for making weapons like ores or food ingredients.
  • Equipment - Weapons and Artifacts are the main way to further enhance your character's combat power in this game. They provide stat boosts and useful effects during combat.

Team Synergy and elemental reactions are central to maximizing your combat experience and accomplishing challenging content.

Story

Players take on the role of the Traveler, a mysterious outsider separated from their twin and stranded in the world of Teyvat. Searching for their lost sibling, the Traveler journeys across different nations, each governed by its own elemental Archon.
Through each region we visit, we become involved in local conflicts, hidden conspiracies, and long-buried secrets tied to powerful gods. We also get to meet with many unique individuals and witness their stories unfold. With every new update, the world expands—introducing new regions, characters, and deeper layers to the overarching storyline waiting for us to discover through exploration and quests.

Conclusion

Genshin Impact is available in PC, Mobile, iOS, and both PlayStation 4 and 5 and also supports cross platform gameplay, allowing you to pick up your progress on your prefered device at any time. I personally recommend this game to people who like open-world games and exploring every corner of the world as genshin will definitely be a rewarding experience. Between the combat system, collecting characters, character building, and to simply play a live-service RPG game, this game has a lot to offer. My favorite part about Genshin is the breathtaking scenery you can discover while exploring through the world paired with amazing ingame music.
